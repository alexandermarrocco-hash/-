Global Shipping

We ship directly from our climate-controlled warehouse. Most orders are processed within 24-48 hours. We offer carbon-neutral shipping options to minimize our environmental footprint.

  • • EU & UK: 3-5 business days
  • • North America: 7-10 business days
  • • Asia: 4-6 business days

Return Policy

Because tea is a perishable food product, we cannot accept returns once the vacuum seal has been broken. If you are unhappy with your order, please contact our support team within 14 days.

quiz Frequently Asked Questions

How should I store my tea?

Keep it in a cool, dark, and dry place. Avoid heat and strong odors. Our bags are designed with extra-thick foil to keep your tea fresh for up to 2 years if unopened.

Is your tea organic?

Most of our small-batch farmers don't pay for expensive international certifications, but they practice traditional organic farming, avoiding chemical pesticides and fertilizers.

Can I modify my order?

Once an order is placed, it is sent immediately to our fulfillment center. If you need to make changes, please contact us via the contact form as soon as possible.

Privacy Policy

At Tea Collective, your privacy is paramount. We only collect the data necessary to process your orders and improve your shopping experience.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your payment data is handled securely by our payment processors and never stored on our servers.

For more detailed information, please contact our data protection officer at privacy@teacollective.com.
